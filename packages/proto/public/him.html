Him

Release Date: September 19th, 2025

Director: Justin Tipping

Producer(s): Jordan Peele, Ian Cooper, Win Rosenfield, Jamal M. Watson

Writer(s): Skip Bronkie, Zach Akers, Justin Tipping

Cinematography: Kira Kelley

Music By: Bobby Krlic

Starring: Marlon Wayans, Tyriq Withrs, Julia Fox, Tim Heidecker

Distributed By: Universal Pictures

Plot Summary: Cameron Cade is a rising quarterback who suffers a potentially career-ending injury after being attacked by an unhinged fan. Just when all seems lost, Cam receives a lifeline when his hero, Isaiah White, offers to train him at an isolated compound. However, as the training accelerates, Isaiah's charisma turns into something darker, sending Cam down a disorienting rabbit hole that may cost him more than he ever bargained for.
