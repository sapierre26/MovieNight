Star Wars

Release Date: May 25th, 1977

Director: George Lucas

Producer(s): Gary Kurtz

Writer(s): George Lucas

Cinematography: Gilbert Taylor

Music By: John Williams

Starring: Mark Hamill, Harrison Ford, Carrie Fisher, Peter Cushing, Alec Guiness

Distributed By: 20th Century-Fox

Plot Summary: The Imperial Forces -- under orders from cruel Darth Vader (David Prowse) -- hold Princess Leia (Carrie Fisher) hostage, in their efforts to quell the rebellion against the Galactic Empire. Luke Skywalker (Mark Hamill) and Han Solo (Harrison Ford), captain of the Millennium Falcon, work together with the companionable droid duo R2-D2 (Kenny Baker) and C-3PO (Anthony Daniels) to rescue the beautiful princess, help the Rebel Alliance, and restore freedom and justice to the Galaxy.
